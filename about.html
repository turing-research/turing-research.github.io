Our Company

Turing Research was born with the vision that research is meant to be taken out of the lab to fuel technological innovations. The company's mission is to advance society and improve people's lives through innovations in science and technology. At the core, we are fueled by our culture of creating research driven breakthroughs for solving challenging practical problems, and mandating our research to drive technology. In this sense, we are different from both research labs and technology companies, and are uniquely positioned to create technology disruptions. We are proud of our science driven vision, people driven mission, and impact driven culture.

We are focussed on solving the transportation and mobility challenge, a growing technological, societal, economical and environmental challenge facing every major city in the world. We are committed to solve this challenge.

Our Founder

Chinmoy Dutta, Founder and CEO

Chinmoy founded Turing Research led by his passion to bridge research and practice. He has years of experience in both industry and academia. Chinmoy specializes in developing highly efficient and scalable advanced optimization algorithms and has made several innovations in mobility algorithms. He has published in leading peer-reviewed conferences and journals and holds several patents.

Prior to founding Turing Research, Chinmoy was a research scientist at Lyft where he played a leading role in promoting science and innovation, building the company's research efforts and creating its academic ties. Previously, he was at Twitter and IBM Research. He did his postdoctoral fellowships at Northeastern University and Max Planck Institute for Informatics in Germany. Chinmoy holds a Ph.D. in Computer Science from Tata Institute of Fundamental Research, Mumbai and a B.Tech. degree in Computer Science and Engineering from Indian Institute of Technology, Kanpur.
